## 1. Positioning & Voice

### 1.1 Who is speaking
One person: Jayant. First-person singular throughout ("I build," "I've shipped," "I work with"). No "we," "our team," "our agency" anywhere on the site, including in metadata, alt text, or footer copy. If a future hire or collaborator is added, this rule is revisited then — not before.

### 1.2 What is NOT on this site (explicitly rejected, do not reintroduce)
These are common in agency-template reference material an AI or freelancer might pull from — they are deliberately excluded here:
- Mission statement section
- Vision statement section
- Core Values section (Ownership / Innovation / Speed / etc.)
- Company origin story / "our journey" narrative
- Dark-gradient "futuristic AI" glow aesthetic
- "Traditional Website vs. AI Website" or "No AI → With AI" comparison graphics
- Fake "Trusted By" logos, placeholder client logos, or "Future Client Logos" sections
- Stock testimonial carousels with invented names/quotes
- Any stat, percentage, or figure not sourced from a real, confirmed data point

### 1.3 One-sentence positioning (CONFIRMED)
> "I build high-converting websites and AI automation systems that help businesses generate more leads and operate more efficiently."

Simple. Honest. Defensible.
